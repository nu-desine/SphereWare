1. Initialise hardware
2. Turn LED blue
3. Wait for button press
4. Check sensors are about 3v, send HID reports
5. Turn LED red if a sensor fails else turn green
6. Wait for button press
7. Set DAC to 0, turn off LED
8. Wait for button press
9. Turn LED red
10. Ramp DAC to 3.3V (4095 steps at 1ms per step)
11. Turn LED white
12. Wait for button press
13. GOTO 7
